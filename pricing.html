Appointment types

I offer flexible counselling options to make getting the support you need easier. I am commited to keeping prices as low as possible for my clients, with prices ranging depending on the service, stricly to cover operational costs associated. The following options are available for booking. In special circumstances I can tailor durations or accessibility to provide a service that can meet your needs.

Secure online telehealth

Experience secure and supportive online counselling from the comfort of your own space. I keep up to date with the latest encrypted technology to ensure the privacy of your sessions. During our telehealth sessions we will work together to understand your concerns and empower your well-being.

Online telehealth sessions for individuals are $130.00 and are 50 minutes in duration.

Text-based therapy

Not everyone can get out of the house and not everyone likes to be on camera. Get confidential support on your own terms with my secure text-based therapy. This is a flexible and accessible approach to therapy, allowing you to connect with me at agreed times, anywhere.

A standard text based session for an individual is $100.00 and is 50 minutes in duration.

In-person sessions

In-person counselling provides a confidential, supportive space for you to explore your thoughts, feelings, and challenges. During our sessions, we will work together to understand your concerns and empower your well-being.

In-person sessions for individuals are $145.00 and are 50 minutes in duration.

Get in touch

If you seek a down-to-earth mental health professional who will take the time to understand, prioritises authenticity and creates a safe space for healing, then I might be a good fit for you. Please contact me if you are interested in learning more or seek an appointment.
