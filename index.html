My History

My non-traditional path to counselling includes two decades in the corporate sector, where I developed analytical skills and a deep interest in human dynamics. My time in Correctional Services further illuminated the complex challenges individuals face, enabling me to help you identify patterns and foster understanding. My personal experiences as a husband, father, and someone who has navigated relationship challenges, complex family dynamics, addiction, and neurodiversity, provide me with genuine empathy and insight. I'm passionate about guiding you through life's overwhelming challenges in a no-pressure environment, helping you unearth the root of your struggles for a more empowered and joyful life. Authenticity is key: I offer a judgment-free space where all topics are welcome, believing true healing stems from complete openness. Together, we'll create a safe place for your true self to thrive.
