Intro

The challenges we face each day can be overwhelming, and navigating them alone can seem impossible. My passion lies in guiding clients in a no pressure environment towards a clear understanding of their struggles. Unearthing the root of these challenges is incredibly freeing, paving the way for a more empowered and joyful life.

Authenticity is the key. There's no judgment here, and no topic is taboo. I believe true healing comes from a place of complete openness and honesty. Society can make us feel as though we we have to wear a mask and not be true to who we are. I'm a guest in your world, and I endeavour to respectfully and deeply understand it. Together, we can create a safe environment where you feel empowered to be authentic, your true sense of self.

Work

My counselling style is rooted in humanism, prioritising a person-centered approach that empowers you to explore your experiences and find your own solutions. My passion is trauma based healing through compassionate presence, free from rigid interventions that may not best suit your unique needs. There are no gimmicks, hidden agendas or lectures here - the science behind human psychology is used to provide complete autonomy of your circumstances. Focus areas I support clients with include, but are not limited to:

  • Mental health and emotional wellbeing
  • Relationships and parenting
  • Child and adolescence
  • Trauma
  • Addiction
  • Career and corporate stress
  • Grief, loss and life transitions

About

My path to becoming a counsellor wasn't traditional. I spent two decades navigating the corporate world, analysing data and leading teams of people. This experience honed my analytical skills, igniting a passion for understanding the human side of things once becoming a people leader â€“ the emotions, experiences, and relationships that make us who we are. My additional background working in Correctional Services highlighted the complex challenges individuals face. I can help you identify your patterns and thought processes while creating a space for understanding and compassion.

Lived experiences as a husband, father, and someone who has exposure to relationship challenges, complex family dynamics, addiction and neurodiversity inform my approach. I understand these struggles firsthand, walking alongside you with empathy and understanding. Peers in my field have described me as 'a balance of masculine and feminine energy'; this I use to assist in gaining clarity and purpose in life for those who seek it.

Services

I like to provide the opportunity to a commitment-free chat for you to ask any questions, this is 5-10 minutes and is at no cost to you as a potential client.

Online Telehealth Experience secure and supportive online counselling from the comfort of your own space, backed by the latest encrypted technology, ensuring privacy of your sessions.
Text-Based Terapy Not everyone can get out of the house. Confidential support on your terms, flexible and accessible, allowing you to connect with me at agreed times, anywhere.
In-Person Sessions A confidential, supportive space for you to explore your thoughts, feelings, and challenges. We will work together to understand and empower your well-being.

All sessions are scheduled for 50 minutes. Location and pricing available at booking.
