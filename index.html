Christopher W. Malone

Master of Counselling, M.A.C.A (Level 2)

Talk-based therapy for individuals and young people

Some roads aren't meant to be travelled alone

The challenges we face each day can be overwhelming, and navigating them alone can seem impossible. My passion lies in guiding clients in a no pressure environment towards a clear understanding of their struggles. Unearthing the root of these challenges is incredibly freeing, paving the way for a more empowered and joyful life.

Authenticity is the key

There's no judgment here, and no topic is taboo. I believe true healing comes from a place of complete openness and honesty. Society can make us feel as though we we have to wear a mask and not be true to who we are. I'm a guest in your world, and I endeavour to respectfully and deeply understand it. Together, we can create a safe environment where you feel empowered to be authentic, your true sense of self.

Confidentiality is paramount

Everything you share in our sessions is kept strictly confidential. I am committed to protecting your privacy. Your story is yours to tell, nobody else's - I use encrypted tools to prevent threats and data loss and meet compliance requirements. All client information is handled according to the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Health Records Act 2001 (Vic).

Accessibility

Sometimes just getting through the day can feel overwhelming, and scheduling an appointment might seem like another hurdle. That's why I offer flexible counselling options to make getting the support you need easier. Whether you prefer in-person sessions, secure online telehealth, or text-based support, I'm here for you. Distance shouldn't be a barrier to receiving help, especially for those living in rural areas.

Therapeutic approach

My counselling style is rooted in humanism, prioritising a person-centered approach that empowers you to explore your experiences and find your own solutions. I integrate other modalities, such as attachment theory or behavioural interventions, to best suit your unique needs. There are no gimmicks, hidden agendas or lectures here - the science behind human psychology is used to provide complete autonomy of your circumstances.
